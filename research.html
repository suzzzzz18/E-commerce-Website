Research and Reference

For the login page of our website, we took the references from Instagram's login page. Instagram's login page looks simple but also clean and elegent. This is the element that pushed us to go for a similiar look because it matched the concept of our website.


Our login page is made using JavaScript and it pops-up when clicked on the login button on navigation-bar. We have altered our login page by adding color to the background and addding elements like a remember me option.

The reference for our navigation bar has been taken from a community built website that provides various kind of informations regarding bonsai and caring for them. We decided to create a similar navigation-bar for our website because it matched our color theme.


Quiet a few elements have been changed in the navigation bar of our website. Factors like the search button, cart button, and login and sign up buttons have been modified to create a slightly different look according to the need od our website.

The footer for our website has also been referenced from the Bonsai Empire website. We liked the structure of the footer and the designing which complemented the website. The footer brought together the whole website and made it look complete.


Although we liked the structure of the footer, we modified a lot of elements to make it match the overall feel of the website and the color theme we had choosen. We also added things like a copyright notice, links to other pages of our website, the services we provide and also a portion for contact us.

Our product page is based on reference from the product page of famous e-commerce website, Alibaba. We browsed through different websites and decided to use Alibaba's product page for the reference because it was the closest to the wireframe we had come up with.


We decided to change the outlook and go for a more simplified look which is why we added three product cards per row to make it look cleaner and also an add to card button to simplify the customer experience. Both the actual and the discounted price are displayed below the products along with the names.

After reviewing different about us sections, we went for a personal portfolio which used designing similar to the one we were searching for.


From the reference about us site we replaced Download CV to View CV and placed it just below the photo to make it more relevant for four people to appear in a single view of laptop screen with borders.

We have selected the bonsai empire blog page section. This website has a very good card design which inspired us to take it as reference.


Likewise, the blog page from reference has similar look but the see more button has been removed to make it clean. And some hover effect with shading and color changing text has been added with making the card bigger when hovering looks mersmerizing.
