How does Technology Affect Daily Life and Business?


Sujina Dangal

January 8, 2023



Developing and advancing quicker than any other innovation in mankind’s history, technology has become an integral part of our lives. It has had a profound impact on every aspect, allowing people to do things faster, easier, and more efficiently than ever before. In the 21st century, we have become so dependent on technology for everything that if it were taken away from us, the world would come to a grinding halt. Technology has permeated every moment of our lives—from the instant we wake up to the very last minute before bed. Every sector, from daily life to education and business to health, is massively influenced by technology. With such immense, wide-reaching effects, it is difficult to overstate its influence.


To begin, it is crucial to acknowledge the vast effects of technology on daily life. In fact, it has become increasingly difficult to find any aspect of our lives not influenced by technology. Technology has enabled us to be more productive and efficient by allowing us to connect with friends and family more easily and enabling us to be more informed and updated with our surrounding news. Technology has made communication more accessible because we can now communicate with people virtually, regardless of their location or time zone. Its development has also granted us greater access to goods and services that simplify many of our day-to-day tasks, which alleviates our load so that we may focus on more urgent and complex tasks at hand.


Moreover, technology’s contribution to the creation of new industries has altered the way we do business, allowing us to tap into the knowledge that was previously only accessible to the few elite classes. By improving communication and providing us with increased databases of knowledge and resources, technology has allowed us to build strong international networks, break down barriers, and foster an environment of collaboration. Storage of more data and information, recorded for the past as well as for real-time statistics, has been beneficial when making informed decisions quickly, which in turn increases productivity. Businesses can now also automate mundane processes and streamline operations with imposing speeds. People can market their products and services more efficiently by providing access to customers all over the world through rapid communication, real-time customer support, and instant customer service with just the click of a button. Technology has opened endless possibilities by increasing many of these opportunities for a multitude of companies that existed during and after its emergence. It has also enabled businesses to stay competitive in the global marketplace by allowing them to monitor the activities of their competitors and react briskly.


Conclusively, from its initial introduction in the early 1990s, technology has had a significant and far-reaching impact on our world. We have seen its transformative power in countless areas, with daily life and business being just two of the greatest affected domains.
