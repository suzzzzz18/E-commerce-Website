Plant Details

Ficus bonsai are trees that are cultivated to be small in size and grown in containers, and they are a popular choice for bonsai enthusiasts because of their attractive foliage and ability to adapt to various styles of bonsai. Ficus come in a range of shapes, sizes, and colors, and they are native to tropical regions of the world. They are generally evergreen, with glossy, leathery leaves that may be oval, lanceolate, or elliptical in shape. To care for a ficus bonsai, you will need to provide it with plenty of sunlight, well-draining soil, and regular watering, as well as pruning and shaping to maintain its desired form. Ficus bonsai can be sensitive to changes in their environment, so it is important to be consistent with their care.
